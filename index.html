Arnab Mondal

Ingenuous, not Ingenious...

I was born and raised at Keshiary, a small town in the state West Bengal in India.

I had my childhood schooling at Keshiary Vivekananda & Saraswati Sishu Mandir (School year 2005) registered under Vidya Bharati.

I passed my class 10th (Madhyamik) from Keshiary High School (School year 2008).

I was awarded Balaram Das Memorial Award for securing top position in class 10th in Keshiary Block.

I went to Midnapore Collegiate School, one of the oldest schools in Bengal as well as India established on 14 November 1834, and passed my 12th (Higher Secondary) from there in 2010.

I got my BE degree in Electrical Engineering (Batch of 2015) from IIEST, Shibpur (Erstwhile "Bengal Engineering College"), 3rd engineering college in India (ranks 21th in NIRF-2020 among Engineering Institutes) empowering the nation since 1856.

I used to participate in an array of extra curricular activities during my school and college life e.g. Spirituality, Recitation, Vocal Arts, Quiz, Impromptu, Debate, Photography, Robotics etc.

I moved to Chennai and joined Lister Technologies as a Software Engineer in the year 2015.

I am now doing MS by Research on the topic "Hardware Software Co-design for Reliable Multi-hop Network Control" from IIT Kharagpur, Institute of Eminence in India, the first of the IITs established in 1951.

I was the Research Consultant from June, 2017 to March, 2021 in a research project sponsored by MHRD and Ministry of Power, Govt. of India, undertaken in Department of CSE, IIT Kharagpur.

Jul 2020: My first publication (Journal-Article) on Cost effective experimental platform for CPS (Cyber-Physical Systems) implementations in IEEE Design & Test Special Issue on CPS Education.

Mar 2021: Our paper Proactive feedback for networked CPS published in Proceedings of the 36th Annual ACM Symposium on Applied Computing. It deals with a generic technique to handle varying end-to-end delays in wireless CPS.

I now live in Kharagpur.

I was awarded "Abritty Visharad" in the year 2007-08 in Recitation.

I was the University Photographer in my undergrad college, IIEST Shibpur (Erstwhile "BESU, Shibpur").

I am a Photographer at La Obscura and a member of The Kolkata Chronicles as a Vocal Artist (Bachik Shilpi).

I watch a lot Sci-Fi movies and a Fan of Marvel Cinematic Universe.

I love watching and playing Cricket the most among all sports. I participated in RSPL 2020 and Inter Hall Cricket (Men) General Championship 2019-20 in IIT Kharagpur.
